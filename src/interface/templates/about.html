About Finshield

Revolutionizing credit risk assessment through alternative data and explainable AI, making financial services accessible to the underbanked population.

The Problem We're Solving

Traditional credit scoring systems exclude 2.5 billion people worldwide from accessing formal financial services. These underbanked populations lack extensive credit histories, making it impossible for conventional models to assess their creditworthiness accurately.

Without access to credit, individuals cannot start businesses, buy homes, or invest in education, perpetuating cycles of financial exclusion and limiting economic growth in emerging markets.

Our Innovative Solution

Alternative Data Integration

Finshield leverages non-traditional data sources including utility payments, mobile phone usage patterns, e-commerce behavior, and digital footprints to build comprehensive credit profiles where traditional data doesn't exist.
